Five Largest Protests in U.S. History

The tradition of protests that demand progressive change in the United States continues.

Here are the country's five top protests in terms of attendance:

  1. George Floyd protests nationwide in 2020 with an estimated 20 million protestors.
  2. Women's March 2017 drew about 5.6 million people nationwide.
  3. March for Our Lives a student led protest in 2017 drew about 2 million people.
  4. Women's March in 2018 drew about 1.5 millioon protestors.
  5. March on Washington for Lesbian, Gay and Bi Equal Rights and Liberationdrew about 1 million people in 2002.
